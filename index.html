Who am I ? An Engineer, Artist & Thinker.

Hi, I'm Lucie, a curious mind driven by abstraction.

I recently graduated with an MEng in Computer Science at Telecom Nancy (FR) and completed an ML Engineer internship at Samsung Electronics. This September, I am moving to Italy to pursue a MSc in Physics of Complex Systems, diving into research at Politecnico di Torino and SISSA.

Lately, I also conducted part-time ML research in collaboration with researchers from CTTC (ES), taught high-schoolers maths for the National Olympiad and co-founded Elrond AI, a B2B startup project that has since wrapped up. I'm also a proud fellow or EuroTech Federation and member of Doctor Without Borders.

Over the last 5 years, I learned about the fundamentals of Maths, Physics, AI, CS, Cybersecurity and Deep Tech. For the next 5 years, I'm focusing on research in Physics of Complex Systems, Neuromorphic Computing and Frontier AI.

My goal is to understand deeply at all scales, fund research and explore nature.
